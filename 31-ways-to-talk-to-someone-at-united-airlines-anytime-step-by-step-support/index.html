When you need help with your travel plans, reaching a live representative at United Airlines can make all the difference. You can call 1-855-742-0149 or 1-800-United Airlines (US/OTA) to speak directly with an agent—available 24/7.

Why Speak to a Live Person at United Airlines?

  • Flight changes or cancellations: Get help adjusting your itinerary or understanding your options if plans change.
  • Booking clarification: Have questions about your reservation? An agent can walk you through it clearly.
  • Refunds and compensation: Our representatives are experienced in handling refund claims and travel disruptions.
  • Technical glitches: For errors during booking or check-in, live support is your best bet for a fast resolution.

Contact Methods for United Airlines Customer Service

  • 📞 By Phone: Call 1-855-742-0149 or 1-800-United Airlines. Say “agent” or press “0” after the prompts to reach a representative faster.
  • 💬 Live Chat Support: Use the live chat feature on the United Airlines website under the Help section.
  • 📱 United Airlines Mobile App: Contact support directly from the app—ideal when you're on the go.
  • ✉️ Email Support: Use email for non-urgent matters to get a documented response.
  • 📲 Social Media Assistance: Message United Airlines on Twitter or Facebook for general questions or basic issues.

Step-by-Step: How to Speak with a Representative

  1. Dial 1-855-742-0149 or 1-800-United Airlines.
  2. Follow the prompts (e.g., “existing reservations”).
  3. Press “0” or say “agent” or “representative” to speak to someone.
  4. Pro tip: Call during off-peak hours to reduce wait times.
